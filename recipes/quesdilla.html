Chicken Quesdilla

Delicious Chicken Quesdilla

Ingredients

  • flour tortilla
  • grilled chicken bits
  • butter
  • shredded cheddar

Steps

  1. add butter to low heat skillet and place bottom tortilla
  2. add chicken bits evenly on the bottom tortilla
  3. add shredded cheese evenly
  4. place another tortilla on top
  5. cook a minute to slight golden brown
  6. flip over and cook to golden brown
  7. place quesdilla on plate and cut to 6 pieces
  8. serve
