Wood Fired Cheese Pizza

Wood Fired Cheese Pizza made from scratch with in house marinina sauce and local mozzerela

Ingredients

  • half corn starch half flour pizza dough
  • in house marinana sauce
  • local shredded mozzerela
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. heat the wood fired oven to proper temp
  2. knead in house premade dough to chosen size
  3. lather olive oil on the dough
  4. add marinana sauce with the edge untouched
  5. add shredded mozzerela on top with edge untouched
  6. bake the pizza for 10 minutes
