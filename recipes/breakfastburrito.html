Red Chile Breakfast Burrito

New Mexican Red Chile Breakfast Bacon Burrito

Ingredients

  • flour tortilla
  • scrambled eggs
  • bacon
  • red chile sauce
  • hashbrown
  • butter
  • shredded cheddar

Steps

  1. place tortilla flat
  2. add scrambled eggs, hashbrown, bacon and cheese in center
  3. wrap the burrito
  4. add butter to low heat skillet and place burrito
  5. cook all sides to slight golden brown
  6. place burrito on plate and pour red chile sauce on top
  7. serve
